Magnificent things are very simple

Hyreus is a platform which aims to uplift unorganised workers in the f orm of regular and stable
employment and protection from exploitation,  which will eventually lead to a better quality of life
for them

Hyreus works by helping unorganised workers in preparing their profiles by collecting and organising
their data, which may include the experiences and skills that the worker has gained from their previous
work, a review from previous employers, their family tree, medical history, schedules and the kind of
work they are interested in.

We combine all these attributes to create the ideal profile for the workers in our database which will
be accessible on our app and website.

Our Database software is programmed to match the employers’ requirements with suitable worker
profiles using criteria like schedules, flexibilities and skill sets. The worker profiles that are best fitted to
the employer’s requirements are automatically suggested to them using different sorting techniques.

After the employer has selected the workers they are interested in hiring, hyreus schedules an interview between the worker and the employer. If the employer chooses to hire the worker, we draw up a contract for the employment terms to be signed by all parties involved, which would include the discussed terms of the employment.
